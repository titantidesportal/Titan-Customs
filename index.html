📋 Rebate & Regulatory Reference ▼ expand
  • 📊 Ad Valorem (Luxury Tax) is calculated on a sliding scale with a maximum of 35%.
  • 🕐 Vehicles over 20 years old may qualify for a 5% rebate on Ad Valorem.
  • 🕐 Vehicles over 40 years old may qualify for a 25% rebate, subject to meeting the prescribed criteria.
  • ✅ Any approved rebate reduces the applicable Ad Valorem (Luxury Tax).
  • 💰 VAT (15%) is calculated on the Added Tax Value (ATV), which includes the Customs Value (FOB), a 10% upliftment, and any applicable customs duty.
  • ⚠️ SARS calculates the final customs charges using the Rand/USD exchange rate (ROE) on the shipment (on-board) date and the applicable Incoterms, which are not included in this estimate.
