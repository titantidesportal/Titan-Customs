📋 Rebate & Regulatory Reference ▼ expand
  • 📦 Total Customs Value is the base SARS uses for all duty calculations — vehicle purchase price + freight to port + insurance costs.
  • 📊 Ad Valorem Excise Duty (Luxury Tax) is calculated on a sliding scale using the SARS statutory formula [(0.00003 × B) − 0.75]% where B is the Total Customs Value, subject to a statutory maximum of 30%.
  • 🕐 Vehicles over 20 years old may qualify for a 5% rebate on Ad Valorem Excise Duty.
  • 🕐 Vehicles over 40 years old may qualify for a 25% rebate on Ad Valorem, subject to meeting the prescribed SARS criteria.
  • ✅ Any approved rebate reduces the applicable Ad Valorem (Luxury Tax) only — Customs Duty and VAT are unaffected unless a full duty rebate is granted.
  • 💰 VAT (15%) is calculated on the Added Tax Value (ATV) = Total Customs Value + Customs Duty + Ad Valorem + CO₂ Levy + 10% upliftment on the Customs Value (mandatory for all non-SACU imports).
  • ⚠️ SARS calculates final charges using the official Rate of Exchange (ROE) on the Shipped On Board (SOB) date — the rate shown here is for estimation only.
